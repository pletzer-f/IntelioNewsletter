02 — Recommendation

Recommended direction
Option A — Night Signal

For a business intelligence product aimed at executives and decision-makers, the electric blue signals digital authority and precision — the same language spoken by Bloomberg, Reuters, and Anthropic. It retains the dark-first philosophy of the current design (dark is right for a morning reading experience) while replacing the yellow with a colour that reads as credible intelligence rather than startup energy.

  • Electric blue (#4F80FF) is instantly associated with data, trust, and precision
  • Warm gold (#E8C75A) is reserved for importance markers in the briefing itself
  • The slight blue tint in the background (#0A0A0F) creates depth vs flat black
  • Works identically in dark mode — no light-mode compromise needed
  • Strong contrast ratios: fully WCAG AA accessible
